Chatroom Rules

This is a tiny personal website with a very small community, so I'm generally trusting you to behave yourselves! If you're unsure, here are some general guidelines.

Currently aiming for things to be PG-13 ish, subject to change.

  1. Be nice.

    Don't harrass or insult people. Hopefully doesn't need explaining.

  2. No offensive language.

    Swearing is fine; bigotry, slurs, and the like will not be tolerated.

  3. No explicit sexual content.

    Includes images, roleplay, or detailed discussion. Vague references, occasional mild jokes or innuendo, etc are usually fine. Absolutely no sexual comments directed at other users.

  4. Avoid excessive serious or heavy discussion.

    This isn't the place for political debates, excessive venting, serious mental health topics, etc.

  5. English only.

    I'm not fluent in any other languages, I need to understand messages to moderate effectively.

  6. Keep it legal.

    This site is hosted in the USA. Don't post anything here that would get me, or you, in serious trouble with the government.

  7. This is not a neutral space.

    This is not a democracy, free speech doesn't apply. You're allowed to have opinions I consider ethically wrong, but I don't have to let you share them.
    I support...
    • The right to gender expression and transition, including access to sports and single-gender spaces.
    • Unrestricted reproductive freedoms.
    • Indigenous rights to land, language, culture, and self-governance. This includes Palestine.
    • Anti-fascism in any form.
    I do not look favorably on right-wing political views, the police, immigration enforcement, or carceral justice systems.

Consequences

For reasonable mistakes, you'll get a warning in chat and/or via email. For anything very obviously not okay (unambiguous bigotry, open harassment, etc) or for ignoring warnings, your account may be banned.

If enough bad behavior is coming from the same IP address, you may receive a blanket IP ban.
